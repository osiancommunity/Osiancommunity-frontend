Refund Policy

Last updated: 30 Nov 2025

Scope

This policy applies to paid quizzes, challenges, and mentorship services purchased on OSIAN.

Eligibility

Refunds may be approved if:
- You were charged incorrectly or duplicate payments occurred.
- A paid quiz or mentorship session was cancelled by OSIAN.
- You experienced verified technical issues preventing participation and support could not resolve them.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are generally not provided for:
- Completed quizzes or delivered mentorship sessions.
- Disqualification due to cheating or policy violations.
- Change of mind or failure to meet participation requirements without prior notice.

Process

To request a refund, email us with your registered email, payment reference, and a brief description. We will assess eligibility and respond within 5–7 business days.

Timelines

Approved refunds are initiated within 7–10 business days. Bank processing times may vary.

Payment Gateway

Refunds for transactions processed via Razorpay will be handled according to Razorpay’s procedures and timelines.

Chargebacks

If a chargeback is initiated, we may suspend related services pending resolution. Please contact support first to avoid disruption.
