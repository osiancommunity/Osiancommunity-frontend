Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 Nov 2025

OSIAN ("we", "us", "our") operates a quiz and mentorship platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

- Account details such as name, email, phone number.
- Usage data such as pages visited, quiz attempts, scores, device and browser information.
- Payment information processed securely by our payment partners (e.g., Razorpay). We do not store full card details on our servers.

How We Use Information

- Provide and improve quizzes, mentorship, and related services.
- Process payments, manage orders, and deliver confirmations.
- Communicate updates, notifications, and support.
- Ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Payments

Payments for paid quizzes and mentorship are processed by Razorpay or other payment gateways. Your payment data is handled according to their security standards. We only receive limited payment metadata required to confirm transactions.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Sharing

We may share data with service providers strictly for operating and improving the platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing). We do not sell personal data.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information. For data requests, contact us using the details below.

Children

Our services are intended for users who meet eligibility requirements under our Terms. If you believe a minor has provided personal data, contact us to remove it.

Changes

We may update this Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform signifies acceptance of the updated Policy.
