Digital Delivery

OSIAN provides digital services such as quizzes, paid challenges, and mentorship. No physical products are shipped. After successful payment, access is delivered digitally through your OSIAN account.

Delivery Timeline

- Paid quizzes: access is granted immediately upon successful payment.
- Scheduled/live events: access is granted prior to the event start time as per event details.
- Mentorship: session details and access are shared via email or dashboard notifications after confirmation.

Order Confirmation

Upon successful order creation, you will receive a confirmation within minutes, along with order details and access information.

Issues with Access

If you do not receive access within the expected time, please contact support with your payment reference. We will assist promptly.
